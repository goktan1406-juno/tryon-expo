Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

tryon ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Photos you upload: Your profile photo and clothing images are collected solely to generate virtual try-on results. Photos are transmitted securely to our AI processing service (fal.ai) and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Profile information: Name and clothing size you optionally provide. Stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data: Basic anonymous usage analytics to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI virtual try-on results using your photos.
  • To display your profile within the app.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your photos to train AI models.

Third-Party Services

We use fal.ai to process virtual try-on requests. Photos sent to fal.ai are subject to their privacy policy. Photos are processed transiently and not retained beyond the duration needed to generate results.

Purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store payment information.

Data Storage

Profile information (name, size) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Try-on result images are stored as links to temporary CDN URLs. We do not maintain a database of your personal information.

Data Retention

Photos sent for processing are deleted from our servers immediately after your try-on result is generated. You can delete your profile and collection at any time from within the app.

Children's Privacy

tryon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your account by contacting us. Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@tryonai.app
